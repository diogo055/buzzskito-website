How We Get There

Position improvement is the #1 driver. Last year with avg position 26-43, most impressions generated zero clicks (pages 3-5 of Google). The new site is already at position 7.7 avg post-migration — page 1 territory. Moving from position 25 to position 5 doesn't just improve CTR linearly — it's exponential. Position 1 gets ~27% CTR, position 5 gets ~5%, position 25 gets ~0.5%.

Seasonal demand multiplier. Search volume for "mosquito control" queries peaks in June-July at 3-5x winter levels. Last year's peak hit 12,400 monthly impressions with position ~35. This year with position ~8 and 3x the indexed pages, we project 25,000+ monthly impressions at peak.

CTR improvement from better titles. New title tags include "$99" and "No Contracts" — these are high-CTR elements that competitors don't have. The old site had generic "BuzzSkito – Free Quotes" titles.
